Consumption Tracker

We consume a lot of media. Smartphones are part of our daily lives, and everyone loves binge watching Netflix. Ever wondered how much time you have actually spent swiping through Twitter or reading that new book on your Kindle? You can track that consumption here in the Media Kitchen.

Devices

Smartphones

Whether you spend your days messaging or just like the occassional game of Candy Crush, keep track of all iOS and Android smartphones.

Tablets

Prefer bigger screens? We also support everything from the iPad to the Surface Pro.

Streaming

Like to binge watch your favorite shows on Netflix? Track all those marathon sessions on your streaming service of choice.

Television

Whether you're a couch potato or you just watch the morning news, keep track of all the time spent in front of the tube.

Computers

We track all laptops and desktops. If you're a PC gamer grinding for XP or a college student cramming for finals, we can track the media consumption, just not the caffeine consumption.

e-Readers

Books aren't dead, they're just on screens now. Perfect for all the bookworms who want to track literature consumption.
